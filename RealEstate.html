Real Estate

Real Estate

The Firm has an in-house, specialised, real estate team which undertakes title verification with elaborate reporting on the same and provides specialised advice on equity and debt transactions, including foreign investments in real estate projects. The Firm’s capability in the real estate sector is comprehensive and includes advising clients on matters pertaining to verifying of title for large parcels of land, for setting of townships, compliance with master plans, building and construction contracts, conveyance, acquisition and disposals, leases, leave and license agreements, joint development agreements, stamp duty, registration and dispute resolution.

The Firm’s clients include various real estate funds, banks and financial institutions (NBFCs), construction-development companies, developers of SEZs, integrated townships, developers of solar power plants, IT parks and several hotels and hospitals.
